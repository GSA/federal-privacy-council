The membership of the Federal Privacy Council includes the Senior Advisor for Privacy at the Office of Management and Budget, who serves as the Chair, and the Senior Agency Officials for Privacy (SAOP) at each agency described in Executive Order 13719, as well other officials from agencies and offices, who are designated by the the Chair.
